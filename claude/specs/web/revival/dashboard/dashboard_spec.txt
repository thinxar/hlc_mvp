DO & BO Level Dashboard - Metrics Design
==========================================

Wireframes: do_dashboard.html, bo_dashboard.html (open in browser)


Terminology
-----------
  Accepted Case : a case where EVERY document is accepted
  Pending Case  : a case with at least one document not yet accepted
  SR            : Service Representative (officer handling revival cases)
  TAT           : Turn-Around Time (days from submission to action)


========================================================================
DO MANAGER — What they care about
========================================================================

A DO Manager oversees 5-20 branches. Their daily concerns:

  "Which branches are falling behind?"
  "Is my division clearing cases fast enough?"
  "Are we going to miss SLA targets this month?"
  "Where should I intervene today?"


1. At-a-Glance Health
   ---------------------------------------------------------
   What       : Division-level totals for the current month
   Metrics    : Total Cases | Accepted | Pending | Acceptance Rate %
   Why        : Instant read on division health without drilling down
   Represent  : 4 headline cards at the top

2. Branch Comparison
   ---------------------------------------------------------
   What       : Side-by-side performance of all branches under this DO
   Metrics    : Per branch — acceptedCases, pendingCases, acceptance%
   Why        : Spot underperforming branches immediately.
                A branch at 60% acceptance needs attention,
                one at 95% does not.
   Represent  : Sorted table or card grid, color-coded by acceptance%
                Red < 70%, Amber 70-85%, Green > 85%

3. Pending Aging — Where is the backlog stuck?
   ---------------------------------------------------------
   What       : Age distribution of pending cases across the division
   Metrics    : Count of pending cases in each age band
                (0-5d, 6-10d, 11-20d, 21-30d, 31-45d, 45+d)
   Why        : Old pending cases (30+ days) signal process failures,
                not just volume. DO Manager needs to escalate these.
   Represent  : Horizontal bar chart, longest bars = most urgent

4. Monthly Trend — Are we improving?
   ---------------------------------------------------------
   What       : Last 6 months acceptance vs pending trend
   Metrics    : acceptedCases, pendingCases per month
   Why        : Shows if the division is trending better or worse.
                A rising pending line = growing backlog problem.
   Represent  : Line chart or grouped bar chart over 6 months

5. Today's Snapshot — What needs action now?
   ---------------------------------------------------------
   What       : Cases submitted today and their current status
   Metrics    : Per branch — today's new cases, accepted, still pending
   Why        : Same-day visibility. If cases submitted in the morning
                are still pending by evening, something is stuck.
   Represent  : Bar chart by branch for today

6. SLA Compliance
   ---------------------------------------------------------
   What       : % of cases processed within target TAT (e.g., 30 days)
   Metrics    : Cases within SLA / Total processed cases
   Why        : Management tracks SLA. DO Manager needs to know if
                their division will meet the target.
   Represent  : Single gauge or % card, with monthly trend line

7. Financial Year View
   ---------------------------------------------------------
   What       : YTD performance for current FY, compared to prev FY
   Metrics    : Cumulative accepted/pending cases, prev FY vs current FY
   Why        : Annual targets. "Are we doing better than last year?"
   Represent  : Two grouped bar charts side by side (prev FY / current FY)


========================================================================
BO MANAGER — What they care about
========================================================================

A BO Manager runs a single branch with 3-5 SRs. Their daily concerns:

  "What is pending on my desk right now?"
  "Which SR is not closing cases?"
  "How long are cases sitting unprocessed?"
  "Am I meeting this month's targets?"


1. My Branch Health
   ---------------------------------------------------------
   What       : Branch-level totals for the current month
   Metrics    : Total Cases | Accepted | Pending | Acceptance Rate %
   Why        : The first thing a BO Manager checks every morning.
   Represent  : 4 headline cards

2. What's Pending Right Now?
   ---------------------------------------------------------
   What       : List/count of currently pending cases with age
   Metrics    : Pending cases count, broken down by age bands
   Why        : Drives daily action. Cases in 0-5 day band are normal
                workflow. Cases in 30+ day band are problems.
   Represent  : Horizontal bar chart (aging bands) +
                optionally a drillable list of old pending cases

3. SR Performance — Who is delivering?
   ---------------------------------------------------------
   What       : Per-SR breakdown of cases handled
   Metrics    : Per SR — acceptedCases, pendingCases, avgTAT,
                cases processed today
   Why        : BO Manager assigns work and reviews SR output daily.
                An SR with high pending and slow TAT needs coaching.
   Represent  : Table sorted by pending desc or acceptance% asc
                Columns: SR | Accepted | Pending | Total | Avg TAT | Today

4. Processing Speed — How fast are we clearing?
   ---------------------------------------------------------
   What       : TAT distribution for processed cases
   Metrics    : Cases processed in 0-5d, 6-10d, 11-20d, 21-30d, 31-45d, 45+d
   Why        : Even if acceptance rate is high, slow processing is a
                problem. A branch clearing everything in 40 days is
                worse than one clearing in 10 days.
   Represent  : Stacked bar chart (monthly trend of TAT bands)

5. Monthly Trend — Am I improving?
   ---------------------------------------------------------
   What       : Last 6 months accepted vs pending
   Metrics    : acceptedCases, pendingCases per month
   Why        : Same as DO level but branch-specific. Shows if the
                branch is improving or deteriorating.
   Represent  : Line or grouped bar chart

6. Today's Work
   ---------------------------------------------------------
   What       : Cases submitted today, accepted today, still pending
   Metrics    : todaySubmitted, todayAccepted, todayPending
   Why        : End-of-day accountability. "Did we clear today's intake?"
   Represent  : Donut or simple 3-card row

7. Financial Year Progress
   ---------------------------------------------------------
   What       : YTD vs prev FY at the same point
   Metrics    : Cumulative accepted/pending, prev FY vs current FY
   Why        : Branch targets are annual. "Am I on track?"
   Represent  : Comparative bar chart
