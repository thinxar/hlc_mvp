Shared Spec - ageingSummary, tatPerformance & perApprover
=========================================================

Reusable document-analysis objects included by:
  - specs/aggregation/active_cases_daily_agg_spec.txt
  - specs/aggregation/active_cases_weekly_agg_spec.txt
  - specs/aggregation/active_cases_monthly_agg_spec.txt

Each aggregation spec produces per-bucket counts of pendingDocuments
and processedDocuments. The objects below break those totals into
age/turnaround bands and per-approver breakdowns.

Bucket bands (shared by both objects)
-------------------------------------
  d0_5     :  [0, 5]   days
  d6_10    :  [6, 10]  days
  d11_20   :  [11, 20] days
  d21_30   :  [21, 30] days
  d31_45   :  [31, 45] days
  d45plus  :  > 45     days


ageingSummary
-------------
Scope:   documents counted in the bucket's pendingDocuments.
Metric:  document_age = bucket_as_of_date - d.uploadedOn   (in days)

  bucket_as_of_date is the snapshot date for the bucket:
    - daily:   calDate
    - weekly:  calWeek
    - monthly: month_end(calMonth)

Field              | Type    | Format / Constraint                                        | Example
-------------------|---------|------------------------------------------------------------|------------
d0_5               | integer | documents with document_age in [0, 5]                      | 12
d6_10              | integer | documents with document_age in [6, 10]                     | 28
d11_20             | integer | documents with document_age in [11, 20]                    | 45
d21_30             | integer | documents with document_age in [21, 30]                    | 30
d31_45             | integer | documents with document_age in [31, 45]                    | 10
d45plus            | integer | documents with document_age > 45                           | 3

Invariant: d0_5 + d6_10 + d11_20 + d21_30 + d31_45 + d45plus == pendingDocuments


tatPerformance (turn-around time performance)
---------------------------------------------
Scope:   documents counted in the bucket's processedDocuments.
Metric:  days_taken = d.actionOn - d.uploadedOn   (in days)

Field              | Type    | Format / Constraint                                        | Example
-------------------|---------|------------------------------------------------------------|------------
d0_5               | integer | documents with days_taken in [0, 5]                        | 520
d6_10              | integer | documents with days_taken in [6, 10]                       | 380
d11_20             | integer | documents with days_taken in [11, 20]                      | 310
d21_30             | integer | documents with days_taken in [21, 30]                      | 140
d31_45             | integer | documents with days_taken in [31, 45]                      | 55
d45plus            | integer | documents with days_taken > 45                             | 15

Invariant: d0_5 + d6_10 + d11_20 + d21_30 + d31_45 + d45plus == processedDocuments


perApprover
-----------
Scope:   documents counted in the bucket's processedDocuments.
Grouping: by d.approvedBy, counting accepted and rejected separately.

Element schema:

Field              | Type    | Format / Constraint                                        | Example
-------------------|---------|------------------------------------------------------------|------------
approvedBy         | string  | officer id from the source document's approvedBy (8-digit)| "57988792"
accepted           | integer | documents with status "accepted" actioned by this approver| 456
rejected           | integer | documents with status "rejected" actioned by this approver| 24

Invariants:
  per-element:  accepted + rejected >= 1 (every emitted element is non-empty)
  per-bucket:   SUM(perApprover[].accepted + perApprover[].rejected)
                    == top-level processedDocuments

Only documents with status in {"accepted","rejected"} contribute.
Pending documents have no approver and are excluded.
Sorted by approvedBy ASC, no duplicate approvedBy values per bucket.
An approver with only accepted (or only rejected) has the other count
at 0 — NOT emitted as two separate elements.
