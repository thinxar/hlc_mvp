<h4 align=Center><i>Endorsement regarding terms and conditions of loans to be placed on the Policy</i></H4>
<Br>
<Br>
<TABLE width=600>
<TR>
	<TD width=25></TD>
	<TD width=550><p style="text-align: justify;">
		Advance(s) by way of loan when granted against the security of the Policy shall 
	    be made by the Corporation on the following terms and conditions;
	</p>
	</TD>
	<TD width=25></TD>
</TR>
</TABLE>
<TABLE width=600>
<TR>
	<TD width=25></TD>
	<TD width=550><OL>
	<LI><p style="text-align: justify;">The Policy shall be assigned absolutely to and held by the Corporation, their successors and assigns as security for the repayment of the advance(s) and of the interest thereon and of all expenses which may be incurred in connection therewith.
</p></li>

	<LI><p style="text-align: justify;">The advance shall not be repaid within a period of six months from the date on which the relative loan is settled.
</p></li>
	<li><p style="text-align: justify;">Interest on the advance(s) shall be paid compounding half-yearly to the Corporation, their successors and assigns at the rate to be specified by the Corporation in respect of each advance when the relative advance is made, the first payment of interest to be made on the date of the next Policy anniversary of on the date six months before the next Policy Anniversary whichever immediately follows the date on which the relative advance is made and every half-year thereafter.
</p></li>

	<li><p style="text-align: justify;">When called upon, repayment of the advance(s) with all interest which may be due thereon  shall  be made on being given  three months notice to that effect.
</p></li>

	<li><p style="text-align: justify;">The Corporation, their successors and assigns shall not be bound to accept  repayment of any of the advance(s) unless tendered in full.
</p></li>

	<li><p style="text-align: justify;">In the event of failure to repay the advance(s) when required or to pay interest on  the due dates  as hereinbefore mentioned or within one calendar month after each due date respectively the Policy shall be held without the necessity of any notice being given to be forfeited to the Corporation, their successors and assigns, And the Corporation shall be entitled to apply the Surrender Value allowable in respect of the Policy in terms of this Regulations and conditions in payment of the advance(s), interest and expenses, the balance, if any, of such Surrender Value to be accounted for to the party entitled thereto.
</p></li>

	<li><p style="text-align: justify;">In case the Policy shall mature or become a claim by death when the amount of the advance(s) or any portion thereof shall remain outstanding, the Corporation shall be entitled to deduct such amount together with all interest upto the date of maturity or of death as the case may be from the Policy moneys, and the balance only shall become due and payable under the Policy.
</p></li></OL>
